Our Services

Maintenance Services

Specialised expertise in crushing and screening plants, plant maintenance, equipment installation, and mobile mining equipment support.

Fabrication & Relining

Quality fabrication, wear protection, and relining services built for demanding mining environments.

Operational Support

We supply experienced operators and provide operational including maintenance from management support for safe and efficient plant operations.

Construction & Setup

Responsive field services and skilled personnel to keep your operation moving safely and efficiently.

Crusher maintenance

Rebuilds, relining, and general maintenance of cone and jaw crushers to improve equipment reliability, maximise performance, and reduce unplanned downtime.

Conveyor belts

Changing conveyor belts, bearings, rollers, and drive units, together with general conveyor maintenance to ensure reliable performance and minimise downtime.

Hydraulic Repairs

Troubleshooting, general maintenance, and hydraulic component change-outs to ensure reliable performance and minimise equipment downtime.

Breakdown Support.

We are on standby 24/7 to provide rapid breakdown support, helping minimise downtime and restore equipment to operation as quickly as possible.

Screen Maintenance

Screen media change-outs, screen rebuilds, and general maintenance to maximise screening performance and extend equipment service life.

Mobile Plant & Equipment Maintenance

General maintenance, servicing, inspections, and repairs of mobile plant and equipment to maximise reliability and minimise operational downtime.

Wear Plate Fabrication

Custom wear plate fabrication and installation solutions engineered to protect high-wear areas and increase equipment longevity.

Feeder Relining

Professional feeder relining services that restore protection against abrasion, reduce maintenance costs, and improve operational efficiency.

Structural Steel Fabrication

Fabrication and installation of structural steel, including safe access ladders, work platforms, and other steel structures to support crushing and screening operations.

Mobile Sandblasting

On-site mobile sandblasting services for surface preparation, corrosion removal, and equipment restoration to extend the life of plant and equipment.

Crusher Maintenance

We service and maintain all types of crushing equipment. Ensures maximum uptime and reduced breakdown risks. Improves efficiency and extends machine lifespan.

Screening Systems Care

Regular inspection and servicing of screening equipment. Prevents blockages, wear, and performance loss. Keeps production flow smooth and consistent.

Conveyor System Support

Maintenance of conveyor belts and drive systems. Reduces unexpected stoppages and material delays. Enhances safety and operational reliability.

Wear Plate Fabrication

Custom fabrication and replacement of wear components. Protects critical machinery from heavy abrasion. Increases durability in harsh mining environments.

Structural Installations

From steel structures to supporting frameworks, our team delivers precise and reliable installations tailored to your operational requirements. We ensure every project is completed safely, efficiently, and to the highest industry standards.

Equipment Installation

We provide professional installation of mining and processing equipment, ensuring all components are correctly aligned, secured, and commissioned. Our focus is on maximizing performance from day one.

Conveyor Installations

Our specialists install conveyor systems designed for durability, efficiency, and seamless material handling. We work closely with clients to minimize downtime and ensure smooth integration with existing operations.

Site Infrastructure Development

We support the development and maintenance of critical site infrastructure, helping create safe, functional, and productive work environments. From new installations to upgrades, we deliver solutions built to last.

Routine Servicing

Regular maintenance keeps your equipment operating at peak performance while reducing the risk of unexpected breakdowns. Our technicians perform thorough inspections and servicing to maximize reliability and uptime.

Breakdown Support

When equipment fails, rapid response is critical. Our team provides efficient troubleshooting and repairs to get your machinery back in operation as quickly and safely as possible.

Preventive Maintenance

Proactive maintenance programs help identify potential issues before they become costly problems. We work to extend equipment life, improve efficiency, and minimize operational disruptions.

Field Repairs

Our mobile service teams are equipped to perform on-site repairs wherever your equipment is located. By bringing our expertise directly to your operation, we reduce downtime and keep your projects moving forward.
